RideContract

RideContracts are digitally enforcable agreements between drivers and riders. Once invoked, the funds are held in escrow until the trip is complete and the dispute window has ended.

Mobile Applications

Driver and Rider native mobile apps provide an interface to the platform whilst abstracting the complexities from the end-user.

Payment Protocol

The multi-payment protocol enables users of the platform to seamlessly pay with and receive any fiat or crypt currency.

Discovery Protocol

Cruzeo uses a fast, secure & scalable off-chain driver matching engine by using distributed hash tables in an encrypted peer-to-peer communication network.

Arbitratiom Model

Disputes & arbitrations are handled in three different tiers: the automatic resolution protocol, a community consensus and an accountable authority.

Reputation Model

We implement a reputation model to reward positive community ehaviour while systematically protecting users from unwanted actors.
